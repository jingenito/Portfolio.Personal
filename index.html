There and Back Again

THE ROAD GOES EVER ON...

"The road goes ever on and on,
Down from the door where it began..."

Welcome to my digital burrow. I am a traveler who has wandered from the towering, snow-capped peaks of the West to settle in the quieter shires of the East coast. Here, I chronicle my adventures, both in the physical realm and the digital ether.

FORGES & THE COSMIC FABRIC

[*] The Digital Forge: When not wandering the woods, I can be found assembling high-end rigs, forging systems with the sheer power of an RTX 4080 to conquer digital realms from Midgar to the Palpagos Islands.

[*] The Weight of the Void: My deeper studies have shifted toward the very fabric of the cosmos—the deep magic of General Relativity. I am diving headfirst into the texts that describe how mass bends the unseen paths of spacetime, deciphering the mathematical runes that govern the motion of the stars.

TALES OF THE BARD

No quest is complete without a song to carry the spirit. My wanderings frequently lead me to the great musical musters of our age—where the thunderous, disciplined ranks of the marching drum and bugle hosts gather in Clifton and Allentown, moving with the precision of ancient, gleaming armies. Yet, of all the melodies that score my travels, it is the sweeping, orchestral mastery of Howard Shore's The Lord of the Rings that remains my truest companion. His music echoes through my halls, weaving a cinematic magic that turns even the quietest days in the Shire into an epic tale.

Within the comfort of my own halls, the minstrelsy never truly fades. Long into the night, I am known to wield a phantom lute—keeping the raucous spirit of rock alive through the digital sorcery of Rock Band Deluxe. Yet, true instruments of brass and wood also call to me; I sound the clarion notes of the trumpet, command the lightning-infused strings of my electric guitar, and have recently begun to master the disciplined, marching cadence of the snare drum.

COMPANIONS OF THE BURROW

Even a seasoned adventurer needs a place to rest. My sanctuary—my own personal Bag-End—is guarded by loyal feline companions, who keep a watchful eye over the household from the towering heights of their grand cat tree.

It is here by the hearth that I can often be found cooking up my next hearty meal, or settled into a comfortable chair, lost in the pages of The Lord of the Rings and diving ever deeper into the lore of Middle-earth. When my mind isn't unraveling the heavy, unseen mysteries of physics, I am resting with a Steam Deck in hand, conquering digital realms. From this quiet burrow, I plot my next great expeditions—whether that means rallying behind the Yankees on the great diamond of the Bronx, or simply leveling up on my current quest.
